# Volume IX, Chapter 5 Dossier: Discrete Theta Terms

## Logical Role

- Role in the monograph: define topological gauge-theory phases depending on
  global bundle/cohomology data and relate them to line spectra.
- Immediate predecessor: confinement, screening, and oblique confinement.
- Immediate successor: anomaly inflow and invertible field theories.

## Definitions And Results

- Finite abelian gauge field as a cocycle.
- Discrete topological action as a class in \(H^D(BA,U(1))\).
- One-form background \(B\) for \(SU(N)/\mathbb Z_N\) bundles.
- Pontryagin-square form of four-dimensional discrete theta terms.
- Line-charge shift by discrete theta parameter.
- Coboundary invariance of the exponentiated action on closed manifolds.

## Claim Ledger

1. Discrete theta terms are cohomology classes of topological actions.
2. On closed manifolds, changing representative by a coboundary does not
   change the exponentiated action.
3. Discrete theta parameters change the genuine line-operator lattice.
4. Boundary variation is part of anomaly inflow data.
