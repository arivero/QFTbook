  forms and higher-form symmetry.

## Definitions And Results

- Extended operator supported on an embedded submanifold with singularity
  prescription.
- Topological defect as isotopy-invariant defect insertion.
- Fusion through tubular-neighborhood collision.
- Endpoints and junctions as lower-dimensional defect operators.
- Higher-form Ward operators acting on charged defects by linking.
- Defect locality and braiding, including Wilson/'t Hooft electric-magnetic
  pairing.
