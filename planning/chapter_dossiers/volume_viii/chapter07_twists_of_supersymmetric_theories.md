# Chapter 07: Twists Of Supersymmetric Theories

## Source Position

Volume VIII now connects the cohomological QFT framework to supersymmetric
field theories.  The chapter supplies the general twisting datum before the
later Witten-Donaldson chapter, where the Donaldson twist and the
Seiberg-Witten low-energy comparison become central.

## Notation Inventory

- `Spin(d)`, `G_R`, `rho`: Euclidean rotation group, R-symmetry group, and
  twisting homomorphism.
- `Spin(d)_rho`: diagonal twisted rotation group.
- `Q`, `d_Q`, `H_Q(A)`: scalar odd symmetry, induced derivation, and
  cohomology of observables.
- `G_{mu nu}`: operator whose `Q`-variation gives the stress tensor in the
  metric-independence criterion.
- `SU(2)_+`, `SU(2)_-`, `SU(2)_R`: groups entering the four-dimensional
  Donaldson twist.
- `A_mu`, `phi`, `bar phi`, `psi_mu`, `chi^+`, `eta`: twisted vector
  multiplet field package.

## Claim Ledger

- Defines a twist as a homomorphism from rotations to R-symmetry and the
  resulting diagonal rotation group.
- Defines cohomological observables by the scalar supercharge complex.
- Proves metric independence under a preserved `Q` Ward identity and
  `Q`-exact stress tensor.
- Gives the Donaldson twist group-theoretic decomposition and the A/B twist
  placement for two-dimensional `N=(2,2)` theories.
- Lists regulator, closure, measure, and contact-term data required before
  cohomological consequences are invoked.
