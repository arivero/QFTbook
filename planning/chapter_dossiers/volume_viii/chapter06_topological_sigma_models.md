# Chapter 06: Topological Sigma Models

## Source Position

This chapter follows cohomological field theories by developing the first
sigma-model examples.  It prepares later chapters on twists of supersymmetric
theories and Witten-Donaldson theory by making the field space, BRST complex,
and localization data explicit.

## Notation Inventory

- `Sigma`: closed oriented Riemann surface.
- `X`: compact Kahler target manifold.
- `phi`: bosonic map from `Sigma` to `X`.
- `psi`, `eta`, `theta`: fermionic variables in A- and B-model complexes.
- `Q`: cohomological differential.
- `M_beta(Sigma,X)`: moduli space of holomorphic maps in class `beta`.
- `ev_a`: evaluation map at a marked point.

## Claim Ledger

- Defines the sigma-model field space and fermionic variables as supermanifold
  coordinates.
- Derives the A-model local-observable complex from de Rham cohomology.
- Identifies the A-model localization equation as the holomorphic-map
  equation.
- Defines B-model local observables as Dolbeault cohomology with polyvector
  coefficients.
- Separates finite-dimensional enumerative localization from the continuum
  QFT construction problem.
