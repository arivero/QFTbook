# Chapter 11: Finite Gauge Theory And State-Sum TQFT

## Source Position

This chapter follows finite-dimensional BV localization by giving a completely
finite TQFT class where field groupoids, path sums, twists, gluing, and
defects can be stated without analytic regularization.

## Notation Inventory

- `G`: finite gauge group.
- `Bun_G(M)`: groupoid of principal `G`-bundles on `M`.
- `omega`: normalized group cocycle defining a Dijkgraaf-Witten twist.
- `f_P`: classifying map of a bundle.
- `K`: triangulation of `M`.
- `g_ij`: lattice flat connection on oriented edges.
- `H(Sigma)`: state space assigned to a closed boundary.
