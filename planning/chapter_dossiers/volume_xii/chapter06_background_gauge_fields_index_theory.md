# Chapter 06: Background Gauge Fields And Index Theory

## Source Position

This chapter follows Hawking radiation by returning to background geometric
fields, now including gauge bundles.  It connects curved-background QFT,
fermion determinants, anomaly descent, and index-theoretic anomaly lines.

## Notation Inventory

- `M^{2n}`: closed oriented Riemannian spin manifold.
- `S^+`, `S^-`: chiral spinor bundles.
- `E`: Hermitian vector bundle with connection `A`.
- `D_A^+`: chiral Dirac operator.
- `Ahat(TM)`, `ch(E)`: characteristic classes in the index theorem.
- `I_{2n+2}`: anomaly polynomial.
- `Det D`: determinant line over the background-field moduli space.

## Claim Ledger

- Defines the coupled chiral Dirac operator and its Fredholm index.
- States the Atiyah-Singer formula with trace-normalization caveat.
- Derives the zero-mode insertion rule from finite-dimensional Berezin
  integration.
- Defines the anomaly polynomial and descent relation for chiral fermions.
- Separates local anomalies as curvature from global anomalies as determinant
  line holonomy.
