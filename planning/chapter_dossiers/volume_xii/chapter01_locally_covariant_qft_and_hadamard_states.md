# Volume XII, Chapter 1 Dossier: Locally Covariant QFT And Hadamard States

## Logical Role

- Role in the monograph: open the curved-spacetime and background-field volume
  with locally covariant functorial QFT and Hadamard state technology.
- Immediate predecessor: flat-spacetime local QFT, stress tensors, anomalies,
  and thermal KMS theory.
- Immediate successor: stress-tensor renormalization, trace anomalies, Unruh
  effect, and Hawking effect.

## Definitions And Results

- Category of globally hyperbolic Lorentzian backgrounds.
- Locally covariant QFT as a covariant functor to algebras.
- Fields as natural transformations from test-function functors to the QFT
  functor.
- Hadamard singular structure for scalar two-point functions.
- State-independent local subtraction and finite local curvature ambiguity.
- Stress-tensor renormalization freedom.

## Claim Ledger

1. Local covariance encodes background restriction and embedding covariance.
2. The Hadamard condition is a microlocal/local short-distance condition on
   states.
3. Products of fields at coincident points require local subtraction.
4. The subtraction ambiguity is local and covariant and is later classified
   by curvature counterterms and conservation constraints.
