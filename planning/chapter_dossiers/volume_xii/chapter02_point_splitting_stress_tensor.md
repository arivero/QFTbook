# Volume XII, Chapter 2 Dossier: Point Splitting And Stress Tensor Renormalization

## Logical Role

- Role in the monograph: develop composite fields and stress tensors from
  Hadamard short-distance structure.
- Immediate predecessor: locally covariant QFT and Hadamard states.
- Immediate successor: trace anomalies, Unruh effect, and Hawking effect.

## Definitions And Results

- Hadamard parametrix \(H(x,y)\).
- Wick square by point-splitting subtraction.

- Trace identity with conformal coupling and local anomaly term.
- Comparison between point splitting and flat-space operator renormalization

## Claim Ledger

1. Wick powers are defined by subtracting the locally covariant singular
   parametrix in all contractions.
2. Stress-tensor renormalization has finite local covariant freedom.
3. Conservation is imposed as a Ward identity after finite local terms are
   fixed.
4. The trace anomaly is the remaining local trace term in the massless
   conformally coupled case.
5. Point splitting is an operator-definition scheme comparable to path-
   integral insertion schemes by finite local terms.
