## Claim Ledger

1. Euclidean smoothness fixes the imaginary Killing-time period
   \(2\pi/\kappa\).
2. Exponential null-ray tracing in collapse gives explicit Bogoliubov
   coefficients with
   \(|\alpha_{\omega\omega'}|^2/|\beta_{\omega\omega'}|^2
   =e^{2\pi\omega/\kappa}\).
3. The continuous \(|\beta_{\omega\omega'}|^2\) contains a \(1/\omega'\)
   normalization density; finite late-time particle numbers require wave
   packets and yield the Planck factor.
4. Late Hawking quanta have exponentially blueshifted precursors on
   \(\mathcal I^-\), which identifies the fixed-background derivation's
   trans-Planckian domain-of-validity issue.
5. The Unruh state is the fixed-background state relevant for outgoing flux
   from collapse.
6. Interacting Hawking radiation requires Hadamard state construction,
   renormalized stress tensor control, and propagation estimates.
