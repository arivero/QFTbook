# Chapter 11: Hydrodynamic Fluctuations And Long-Time Tails

## Source Position

This chapter follows nonequilibrium steady states and open-system limits by
developing the stochastic and Schwinger-Keldysh fluctuation layer of
hydrodynamic effective theory.

## Notation Inventory

- `varphi_A`: hydrodynamic fluctuation variables.
- `chi_AB`: static susceptibility matrix.
- `D`, `sigma`: diffusion constant and conductivity.
- `xi^i`, `Xi^{ij}`: stochastic current and stress-tensor noise.
- `n_r`, `n_a`: Schwinger-Keldysh `r,a` density variables.
- `G_R^{JJ}`: retarded current correlator.
- `Lambda_hyd`: hydrodynamic cutoff.
