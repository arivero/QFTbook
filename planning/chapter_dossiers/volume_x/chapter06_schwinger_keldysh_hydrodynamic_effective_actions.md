# Chapter 06: Schwinger-Keldysh Hydrodynamic Effective Actions

## Source Position

This chapter follows hydrodynamics from Ward identities by constructing a
real-time doubled effective action for charge diffusion.  It connects the SK
generating functional, KMS condition, noise positivity, and hydrodynamic
retarded poles.

## Notation Inventory

- `A_1`, `A_2`: background gauge fields on the two SK contours.
- `A_r`, `A_a`: average and difference sources.
- `varphi_1`, `varphi_2`: hydrodynamic phase fields.
- `B_s`, `B_r`, `B_a`: gauge-invariant source-phase combinations.
- `chi`, `sigma`, `D`: susceptibility, conductivity, and diffusion constant.
- `I_diff`: quadratic diffusion effective action.

## Claim Ledger

- Defines doubled source variables and unitarity condition `Z[A,A]=1`.
- Defines hydrodynamic phase variables and gauge-invariant combinations.
- Constructs the quadratic diffusion action with positive imaginary part.
- Derives the diffusion equation and retarded density correlator.
- States the dynamical KMS transformation and uses it to fix the noise
  coefficient.
