# Issue #264 Vafa-Witten Hypotheses Audit

## Scope

- Updated the Vafa--Witten positivity paragraph in the global anomalies,
  spontaneous symmetry breaking, and pions chapter.
- Updated the chapter dossier symbol and claim ledgers.

## Content Added

- Introduced `hyp:vafa-witten-measure-data`, making explicit that the argument
  requires:
  - a gauge-invariant Euclidean vectorlike gauge-field measure with positive
    quark mass;
  - defined expectations for the fermion bilinears and source deformations
    being used;
  - reflection positivity sufficient for Osterwalder--Schrader reconstruction;
  - anti-Hermitian Dirac operator pairing and nonnegative determinant;
  - existence of the infinite-volume and small-mass limits used to select a
    phase.
- Added a direct status statement that, at finite lattice cutoff, these are
  properties of the chosen regulator, while in four-dimensional continuum QCD
  the required measure and scaling limits are part of the open construction
  problem.
- Rephrased the Vafa--Witten conclusion as conditional on these hypotheses and
  as a constraint on vector-flavor-breaking orientations, not as a derivation
  of the chiral condensate.

## Verification Target

- The issue requested addition of the reflection-positivity hypothesis and the
