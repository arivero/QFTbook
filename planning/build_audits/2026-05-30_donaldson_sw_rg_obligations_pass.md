# Donaldson-Seiberg-Witten RG-Obligations Pass

Date: 2026-05-30

## Scope

This pass advances issue #698 in Volume VIII, Chapter 8.  The target is the
Witten Donaldson-Seiberg-Witten comparison datum, specifically the risk that
the displayed comparison formula hides the actual QFT/RG mechanism.

## Changes

- Added a proof-obligations paragraph immediately after the comparison
  hypothesis.
- The text now lists the distinct ingredients required for a genuine QFT
  derivation:
  1. nonperturbative construction of the twisted pure \(\mathcal N=2\) gauge
     theory with \(Q\), descent observables, gauge-fixed measure, and ASD
     compactification boundary data;
  2. \(Q\)-compatible Wilsonian flow to the Seiberg-Witten Coulomb-branch
     family, including the Abelian contact term \(T(u)\);
  3. control of the regular \(u\)-plane integral and its chamber boundary
     contribution;
  4. replacement of singular-fiber neighborhoods by twisted Abelian monopole
     theory with orientation and determinant-line phases;
  5. derivation of the point normalization, Gaussian contact term, comparison
     phase, and Moore-Witten power of \(2\) from the same regularized
     conventions.

## Proof-Form Status

The comparison formula remains a hypothesis/QFT target datum in the monograph.
This pass makes the hidden proof tasks explicit rather than presenting the
formula as a completed physical derivation.
