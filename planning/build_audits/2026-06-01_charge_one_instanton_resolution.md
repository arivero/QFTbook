# Charge-One Instanton Resolution Pass

This pass continues GitHub issue #629.  The prior singular-instanton text
distinguished Uhlenbeck, Gieseker, and Donaldson--Uhlenbeck compactifications
and proved the positive-ADHM-moment-map stability mechanism.  The new pass
makes the first nontrivial resolution map explicit rather than leaving
``point instanton'' as an unexpanded phrase.

Added manuscript content in Volume VII Chapter 16:
- the charge-one stable ADHM quotient
  \((I,J)/\mathbb C^\ast\simeq T^\ast\mathbb P(W^\ast)\);
- the Uhlenbeck moment-map morphism
  \(([I],J)\mapsto \nu_{I,J}=J\otimes I\in\mathfrak{sl}(W)\);
- the identities
  \(\operatorname{Tr}\nu_{I,J}=I(J)\),
  \(\nu_{I,J}^2=I(J)\nu_{I,J}\), and
  \(\operatorname{rank}\nu_{I,J}\le 1\), so \(I(J)=0\) maps to the closure
  of the minimal nilpotent orbit;
- the interpretation of the zero section \(J=0\) as the exceptional locus
  mapped to the Uhlenbeck small-instanton apex.

The paired calculation check now verifies the trace, nilpotence, rank-one
minor, and dimension/codimension arithmetic for this charge-one
Gieseker-to-Uhlenbeck map.
