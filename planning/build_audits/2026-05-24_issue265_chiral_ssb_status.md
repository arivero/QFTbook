# Issue #265 Chiral SSB Status Audit

## Scope

- Updated the QCD chiral-symmetry-breaking entry point in the global anomalies,
  spontaneous symmetry breaking, and pions chapter.
- Updated the chapter dossier symbol and claim ledgers.

## Content Added

- Added `conj:qcd-chiral-symmetry-breaking`, stating the order of limits and
  the nonzero chiral condensate condition
  \[
    \lim_{m\downarrow0}\lim_{V\to\infty}
    \langle \bar q_{R,J}q_{L,I}\rangle_{m,V}
    =
    \Sigma_0\delta_{IJ},\qquad \Sigma_0\neq0 .
  \]
- Stated that the unbroken continuous nonabelian flavor symmetry is the
  diagonal vector subgroup \(SU(N_f)_V\) only under this dynamical input.
- Added the status statement that this is not presently a theorem derived from
  the four-dimensional continuum QCD Lagrangian or from a completed lattice
  continuum limit.
- Stated that lattice simulations and phenomenology are evidence, while
  semiclassical instanton calculations do not supply a controlled infrared
  derivation of the condensate.

## Verification Target

- The issue requested that chiral SSB be flagged as a conjectured property of
  QCD.  The manuscript now gives it a named conjecture and makes the pion EFT
  explicitly conditional on that phase.
