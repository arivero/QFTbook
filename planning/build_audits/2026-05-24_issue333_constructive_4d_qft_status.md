## Mathematical Boundary

- A constructive solution is stated as Wightman/OS or equivalent local-net data
  with positivity, covariance, locality, and the spectral properties used
  later.
- Low-dimensional constructive examples are separated from four-dimensional
  scalar triviality, finite regulators, perturbative expansions, and open
  four-dimensional gauge/scalar construction problems.
- Free and generalized-free four-dimensional examples are not treated as
  solutions of the interacting scalar/gauge constructive problem.
