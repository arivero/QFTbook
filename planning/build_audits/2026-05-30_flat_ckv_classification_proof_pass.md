# 2026-05-30 Flat CKV Classification Proof Pass

## Scope

This pass continues issue #691 by reading a short proof that was surfaced by
the theorem/proof scan: the classification of flat conformal Killing vectors
in Volume III, Chapter 2.

## Decision

The statement was not demoted.  It is a genuine local classification theorem:
in \(D\geq3\), the conformal Killing equation closes to a finite-dimensional
solution space generated by translations, rotations, dilatations, and special
conformal vector fields.  The problem was not false theorem-family status but
a compressed proof.

## Manuscript Change

- Expanded the proof after the conformal factor rigidity lemma.
- Displayed the constant-Hessian equation and introduced the quadratic
  special-conformal piece \(q_\rho=c_\rho x^2-2(c\cdot x)x_\rho\).
- Explained why subtracting \(q_\rho\) leaves a vector field with vanishing
  Hessian and therefore only affine constants on a connected domain.
- Reimposed the first-order conformal Killing equation to fix the symmetric
  part of \(B_{\mu\nu}\).
- Added the converse check identifying the Weyl factor of the translation,
  rotation, dilatation, and special conformal pieces.
