## Mathematical Boundary

- The QED statement asks for flux-labelled charged asymptotic spaces, wave
  operators into charged physical Hilbert sectors, compatibility with
  Gauss-law flux superselection, and agreement with inclusive detector
  probabilities.
- The QCD statement asks for physical hadronic asymptotic spaces, isometric
  wave operators, treatment of resonances through analytic scattering data,
  inclusion of massless Goldstone modes in chiral limits, and detector limits
  on the same scattering domain.
- Both statements are formulated as open problems, separate from
  Bloch--Nordsieck/KLN cancellation, Faddeev--Kulish perturbative dressings,
  asymptotic freedom, factorization, mass gap, and confinement hypotheses.
