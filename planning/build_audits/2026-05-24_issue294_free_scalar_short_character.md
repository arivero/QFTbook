# Issue #294 Free Scalar Short-Module Character Pass

## Scope

  `[Vol V Ch 4] Free-scalar partition: EOM-null-state subtraction not connected to Ch 7 shortening`.
- Target chapters:
  `monograph/tex/volumes/volume_iii/chapter04_radial_quantization_and_state_operator_correspondence.tex`

  `thm:scalar-unitarity-bound-shortening`.

## Content Added

- Added Proposition `prop:free-scalar-letter-short-module-character`.
- Defined \(\widehat P^2=\delta^{\mu\nu}\widehat P_\mu\widehat P_\nu\) and
  identified \(\mathbf 1\) as the trivial \(SO(D)\) representation.
- Derived the unconstrained scalar generalized Verma character
  \(q^\Delta/(1-q)^D\) from the PBW basis of commuting translations.
- Verified directly from the conformal commutators that
  \(\widehat P^2|\phi\rangle\) is singular at
  \(\Delta_\phi=(D-2)/2\).
- Interpreted the descendants of \(\widehat P^2|\phi\rangle\) as the traced
  symmetric derivatives, with character \(q^{\Delta_\phi+2}/(1-q)^D\).
- Used the Fischer decomposition of homogeneous polynomials to identify the
  quotient with harmonic polynomials, equivalently symmetric traceless
  derivatives of the free scalar.
- Updated the Chapter 4 and Chapter 7 dossiers so the character subtraction is
  recorded as the representation-theoretic consequence of scalar shortening.
