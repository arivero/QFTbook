# QFT Transcription Outline

This file summarizes the transcription layer for planning. It is not a table
of contents for the finished monograph.

## First Sequence: Local Fields, Green Functions, And Scattering

Source file:

- `transcription/tex/253a/foundations.tex`

Planning topics:

- opening local quantum data;
- relativistic particles and Poincare representations;
- causality, local fields, Poincare covariance, and microcausality;
- Lagrangian and Hamiltonian mechanics;
- time evolution and path-integral time slicing;
- correlation functions and Wick rotation;
- Gaussian integrals and Wick contractions;
- perturbative expansions in quantum mechanics;
- scalar field theory and canonical quantization;
- Noether currents and stress tensor;
- scalar path integrals and Green functions;
- spectral representation and particle content;
- perturbative correlation functions;
- Lorentzian Green functions;
- Haag--Ruelle-type asymptotic-state logic;
- LSZ reduction;
- S-matrix, probabilities, cross sections, partial waves;
- massive spin;
- Dirac fields, fermionic statistics, and spinor structures;
- Grassmann path integrals;
- massless particles, helicity, and gauge redundancy;
- Maxwell theory, constraints, and gauge fixing;
- QED and radiative corrections.

Monograph use:

- mostly Volume I;
- selected renormalization and QED material continued in Volume II.

Ordering constraints:

- spectral representation appears before scattering;
- perturbative Green functions appear before the nonperturbative S-matrix;
- perturbative S-matrix diagrams follow LSZ.
