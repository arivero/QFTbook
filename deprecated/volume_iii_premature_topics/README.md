# Deferred Volume III Topics

These files were moved out of the compiled monograph on 2026-05-21.

Reason: the compiled manuscript should not rush from core QFT and RG into
advanced CFT, supersymmetry, localization, defects, holography, or
bootstrap-specific technology before those subjects have been developed from
their own foundations.

Deferred topics in this folder include:

- conformal blocks, projective-lightcone machinery, and bootstrap methods;
- surveys of CFT landscapes;
- two-dimensional Virasoro and modular material;
- four-dimensional superconformal and \(\mathcal N=4\) material;
- large-\(N\), spin-chain, duality, conformal-manifold, and holographic
  chapters;
- Witten diagrams, Mellin amplitudes, localization, integrated correlators,
  and defects.

These drafts may be reused later only after a fresh source and rigor pass.
